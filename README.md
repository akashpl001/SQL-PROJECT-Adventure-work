### Q1. Customer Contact Information
Retrieves customer **email address, phone number, and phone type** by joining multiple person-related tables.

**Business use case:** Customer profiling and contact management.

---

### Q2. Sales Orders in May 2011
Fetches all sales order details placed during **May 2011**.

**Business use case:** Monthly transaction review.

---

### Q3. Sales Details Using Order Header Date
Retrieves sales order details for May 2011 using the **SalesOrderHeader** table for accurate date filtering.

**Business use case:** Reliable time-based sales reporting.

---

### Q4. Total Units Sold in May 2011
Counts the total number of products sold during May 2011.

**Business use case:** Monthly sales volume analysis.

---

### Q5. Monthly Sales Trend for 2011
Displays total units sold per month for the year **2011**, ordered chronologically.

**Business use case:** Yearly sales trend and performance tracking.

---

### Q6. Customer-Level Sales Analysis
Calculates total sales amount for a specific customer (**Gustavo Achong**).

**Business use case:** Customer revenue contribution analysis.
